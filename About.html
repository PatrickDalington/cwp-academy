About Us

Introducing CWP ACADEMY - Unleash Your Coding Potential

At CWP ACADEMY, the acronym for "CodeWithPatrick," we're more than just a coding academy. We're a thriving community of passionate learners, led by our visionary founder, Olumba Patrick, a seasoned software engineer with over six years of industry experience.

But Patrick's expertise doesn't stop at coding. He's a music enthusiast and a virtuoso in playing three musical instruments, with the piano as his forte. Just as music harmonizes melodies, we aim to harmonize your coding journey, making it both educational and enjoyable.

Established in 2021, CWP ACADEMY is your gateway to the world of programming. We offer comprehensive courses in various programming languages, equipping you with the skills and knowledge to excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Our legacy speaks for itself - having nurtured and mentored hundreds of students over the years, we're committed to shaping the next generation of proficient programmers. Our goal is clear: to empower you to tackle complex challenges with innovative solutions that will change the world.

Join CWP ACADEMY, where coding meets creativity, and together, let's script a brighter future.
